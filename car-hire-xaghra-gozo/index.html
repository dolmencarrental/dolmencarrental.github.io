Explore Gozo's heritage from your Xagħra base

Xagħra sits on a hilltop in the north-east of Gozo, wrapped around a pretty village square and famous for the Ġgantija megalithic temples — older than Stonehenge and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Just below the village lies Ramla Bay, the island's most beautiful red-sand beach, along with the legendary Calypso Cave viewpoint. It is a magical area to stay, and a car turns those scattered highlights into an easy morning's drive.

Because many Xagħra rentals are farmhouses and villas tucked down country lanes, having the car delivered to the door is a real relief. Send us your dates and address on WhatsApp; we confirm what is available and bring the car to you, keys ready. At the end of your holiday we collect from the same spot — no fees, no detours to a rental office.

Around Xagħra by car

  • Ramla Bay in minutes. The red-sand beach and Calypso Cave viewpoint are a short drive downhill.
  • Culture on your doorstep. Ġgantija temples and the village windmill are walkable, the rest of Gozo a quick drive.
  • Rural roads. Country lanes are narrow — a compact car or a higher SUV both work well; we will advise for your villa.
  • Free door delivery and collection included, even down the quieter lanes.
