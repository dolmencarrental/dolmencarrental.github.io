Book without the usual hassle

Renting a car often means a large security deposit frozen on your credit card, or being turned away because you only have a debit card. We do things differently. No-deposit hire — questions

Do I really pay no deposit?

Correct — there is no security deposit and nothing frozen on your card. You reserve on WhatsApp and pay the rental amount when you collect the car.

Can I book without a credit card?

Yes. A credit card is not required to reserve a car with us. A quick WhatsApp message with your dates is all we need.

When and how do I pay?

You pay when we deliver the car and hand over the keys, at the Mġarr ferry or your accommodation. We will agree accepted payment methods when you book.

Is insurance still included with no deposit?

Yes — standard insurance, unlimited mileage and 24/7 roadside assistance are included in the price, exactly as with any of our rentals.
