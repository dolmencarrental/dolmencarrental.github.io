Ta Randu Farmhouse BnB on Gozo

Nice to know

Just a short walk/drive from Hondoq ir-Rummien Bay

Visit Saint Mary on the Rocks church - it's a great church with a view!

St. Joseph's festa: Experience Qalas biggest celebration in July. It is held over 10 days from end of July to early August. Enjoy Bands, fireworks and the best possibility to experience local culture.

Qala is home to ancient cart ruts (mysterious tracks carved into the rocks)

Qala’s main square is the heart of social life, with restaurants and bars open year-round. Guests can enjoy a meal or a drink here, and if they’re lucky, catch a weekly music jam session at Zeppi’s Pub.

Qala is just a 5-minute drive from the Gozo ferry terminal at Mgarr, and buses connect it to Victoria (Gozo’s capital) from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM. The “Buttigieg” bus stop is a handy reference point for guests exploring the island.
