Can you hire a car on Gozo?

Yes, you can hire a car on Gozo. The island has local car rental companies, and hiring is by far the easiest way to explore — Gozo's beaches, temples and villages are spread out and bus services can be slow and infrequent, especially in the evenings. With Dolmens Garage you do not even need to visit a rental office: we deliver the car free to the Mġarr ferry terminal or your accommodation, and you pay when you pick it up. A quick WhatsApp message is all it takes to book.

Do I need an international driving licence?

If you hold a valid driving licence from your home country (in English or with an official translation) you can drive on Gozo. EU driving licences are accepted without any further requirement. For licences issued outside the EU we recommend carrying an International Driving Permit (IDP) alongside your national licence, though it is not always required. If you are unsure, send us a message and we will confirm for your specific licence.

What is the minimum age to rent a car on Gozo?

With us the minimum age is 21, and you must have held your driving licence for at least one year. If you are travelling with younger drivers in your group, let us know and we will advise.

Driving on Gozo — what to expect

  • Drive on the left. Like the rest of Malta, Gozo drives on the left. It becomes natural quickly, but take it easy for the first hour.
  • Roads are narrow and scenic. Village streets and country lanes can be tight and winding — go slowly and watch for pedestrians and cyclists.
  • The island is small. Almost everywhere is within 15–20 minutes of Victoria, the central capital.
  • Hills and junctions. Gozo is hillier than people expect; an automatic makes hill starts and junctions more relaxed if you prefer.
  • Fuel. There are several fuel stations around the island. We provide the car with a full tank and ask you to return it full.

Getting your car — the ferry and delivery

Most visitors arrive on the passenger ferry from Ċirkewwa (Malta) into Mġarr Harbour on Gozo. You do not need to bring a car across: we meet you at the terminal with your rental so you drive off the moment you land. If you would rather collect at your hotel or villa, we deliver there instead — anywhere on Gozo, free of charge. If you do bring your own car on the ferry, we can arrange the handover at the port too.

Parking on Gozo

Parking is generally easy and mostly free across the island. It gets busier in summer around popular spots like Victoria, Xlendi and Marsalforn — a compact car helps, and we are always happy to share the handiest places to park for wherever you are headed.

How much does it cost?

Rates depend on the season, how long you rent and the size of car, but ours are among the most competitive on Gozo and always include insurance, unlimited mileage, roadside assistance and free delivery — with no deposit.
